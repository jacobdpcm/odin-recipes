Bread Egg

Description:

This is a unique dish that involves cooking an egg within a slice of bread.

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Egg
  • Oil
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Begin heating the pan and add oil.
  2. Cut a hole into a slice of bread, and place both the slice and the cutout into the pan.
  3. Crack an egg into the hole in the slice. As both sides cook add the cheese.
