Chmkin Adobo

Description:

This is a classic Phillipino dish that focuses on enriching the flavor with vinegar.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce

Steps:

  1. Mix the Soy Sauce and Vinegar together.
  2. Add all ingredients to pot and sear.
  3. Strain the fat, then cover pot and leave in oven for 1 hour.
